Welcome to my text-based Monopoly Game!
This is a project I started in 8th grade as something fun and random,
and has evolved into a full website!
To play, simply click the button below and follow the prompts!
To save a game, copy the numbers following /play/ in the URL and save that hash.
To return to a saved game, go to https://text-based-monopoly.herokuapp.com/play/
followed by your saved hash.
Saves are kept for 2 weeks.

Enjoy :)
